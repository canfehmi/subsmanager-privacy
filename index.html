Privacy Policy

Subs Manager respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal data. This application is designed to work primarily with locally stored data and does not require user accounts.

Information We Collect

Subs Manager does not require account creation and does not collect personal identification information. All subscription data entered by users is stored locally on the device.

The app may store the following information locally:

  • Subscription names, prices, and billing cycles
  • Categories and notes entered by the user
  • User preferences such as language and appearance
  • Premium purchase status

We do not collect names, email addresses, phone numbers, or contact information.

In-App Purchases

Payments and subscriptions are processed securely by Google Play. Subs Manager uses RevenueCat to manage purchase verification and entitlements.

We do not store, access, or process payment details such as credit card numbers or billing information.

Data Usage

All locally stored data is used only to provide core app functionality, including:

  • Managing and displaying subscriptions
  • Providing premium features
  • Restoring purchases
  • Improving app usability and stability

Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share user data with third parties.

Limited data may be shared only with the following services for essential functionality:

  • Google Play Services (for billing and purchases)
  • RevenueCat (for subscription management)

This data is used strictly for purchase verification and entitlement management.

Data Storage and Security

User data is stored locally on the device using secure storage mechanisms. We take reasonable measures to protect stored data.

However, no electronic storage method is completely secure, and absolute security cannot be guaranteed.

User Data Control and Deletion

Users have full control over their data.

  • All data can be deleted at any time using the "Clear All Data" option in the app settings.
  • Uninstalling the application will also remove all locally stored data.

Since no cloud storage is used, deleted data cannot be recovered.

Children’s Privacy

Subs Manager is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. Any changes will be published on this page.
