Privacy Policy

Subs Manager respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal data.

Information We Collect

Subs Manager does not require account creation. The app may collect limited information to provide core functionality, including:

  • Subscription information entered by the user
  • App usage data for performance and analytics
  • Purchase and entitlement data processed through Google Play and RevenueCat

We do not collect sensitive personal data such as names, email addresses, or contact information.

In-App Purchases

Payments and purchases are processed securely by Google Play. Subs Manager uses RevenueCat to manage purchase status and entitlements. We do not store or process payment information directly.

Data Usage

Collected data is used solely to:

  • Display and manage subscriptions
  • Restore purchases
  • Improve app stability and performance

Data Sharing

We do not sell or share user data with third parties. Data may be shared only with:

  • Google Play Services
  • RevenueCat

This data sharing is strictly limited to purchase verification and core app functionality.

Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect user data. However, no method of electronic storage or transmission over the internet is completely secure.

User Rights

Users can delete all locally stored app data at any time by uninstalling the application.

Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page.
